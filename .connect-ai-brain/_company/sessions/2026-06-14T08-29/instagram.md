📸 Instagram Agent: New Profile Picture and Consulting Setup 📸

**Task:** Set up a new profile picture and consulting settings for the company's Instagram account.

**Goal:** Create a professional and visually appealing profile picture that accurately represents the company's brand and values, while also setting up consulting settings to optimize engagement and conversion rates.

**Target Audience:** Young adults aged 18-35 interested in technology, entrepreneurship, and innovative ideas.

**Profile Picture:**

1. **Design:** Use a modern and minimalist design that incorporates the company's logo and color scheme.
2. **Size:** Ensure the profile picture is optimized for various devices and platforms (e.g., desktop, mobile, tablet).
3. **Caption:** Add a brief description of the company's mission, values, or key services to provide context.

**Consulting Settings:**

1. **Story Insights:** Enable story insights to track engagement metrics, such as swipes, taps, and clicks.
2. **Instagram Shopping:** Set up Instagram shopping to tag products and enable customers to purchase from the feed.
3. **Reels:** Create reels that showcase company culture, employee spotlights, or behind-the-scenes content.

**Assign Tasks:**

1. **VIDEO PLANNER**: Design a profile picture that incorporates the company's logo and color scheme.
2. **TREND SNIPER**: Analyze Instagram trends and insights to inform consulting settings and optimize engagement.
3. **WRITER**: Craft a compelling caption for the profile picture that accurately represents the company's brand.

**Timeline:**

1. **Day 1-2:** Create the new profile picture and set up consulting settings.
2. **Day 3-4:** Analyze Instagram trends and insights to optimize engagement and conversion rates.
3. **Day 5:** Implement reels and shopping features to enhance customer experience.

**Evaluation Criteria:**

1. **Profile Picture:** Ensure the profile picture accurately represents the company's brand and values, while also being visually appealing.
