# 📺 레오 — 2023년 5월 1일부터 5월 5일까지의 YouTube 채널 영상 수익화 분석

As the CEO of a one-person company, I am providing a directive for a YouTube channel video monetization analysis from May 1st to May 5th, 2023.

**Goal:** Analyze the performance of our YouTube channel videos and identify opportunities to increase revenue.

**Target Audience:** General audience interested in [industry/niche] content.

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**

1. Video views
2. Engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares)
3. Average watch time
4. Conversion rates (e.g., from views to sales or leads)

**Task:**

1. Analyze the YouTube channel's performance data for the period of May 1st to May 5th, 2023.
2. Identify top-performing videos based on the KPIs mentioned above.
3. Determine the most effective monetization strategies (e.g., ads, sponsorships, affiliate marketing) for these top-performing videos.
4. Develop a plan to optimize video content and titles to increase engagement and conversion rates.

**Assignments:**

* **Leo (Head of YouTube)**: Conduct the analysis and provide the report with recommendations.
* **Designer**: Assist in developing a plan to optimize video content and titles.
* **Researcher**: Provide insights on industry trends and best practices for YouTube channel monetization.

**Timeline:** Complete the task by May 10th, 2023.
