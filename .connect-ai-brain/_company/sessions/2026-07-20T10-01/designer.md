**목표:** 유튜브 채널을 최적화하여 브랜드 인지도를 개선하고, 경쟁사와 차별화하는 디자인을 개발합니다.

**작업 분배:**

1. **Designer**: 브랜드 아이덴티티(Logo, Typography) 및 썸네일 컨셉 3안을 설계하겠습니다.
2. **Developer**: YouTube 채널 코드를 작성하여 채널의 성격을 반영하는 디자인을 구현할 것입니다.

**산출물:**

1. **Designer**:
	* 브랜드 아이덴티티(Logo, Typography) 3종
	* 썸네일 컨셉 3안 (Image + Caption)
2. **Developer**:
	* YouTube 채널 코드 (HTML, CSS, JS)

**Timeline:** 24 hours

**Resources:**

1. `youtube_account.json` - Contains API keys and channel information
2. `trend_sniper.py` - Trend analysis script
3. `branding_guidelines.md` - Branding guidelines document

**Evaluation Criteria:**
The success of this task will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

1. **Branding Consistency**: The branding design should be consistent with our company's values and mission.
2. **Unique Design**: The design should be unique and differentiate our channel from others.
3. **Effectiveness**: The design should effectively communicate our brand message and resonate with our target audience.

**Next Step:**
Once the design is complete, we will conduct a thorough review and feedback session to ensure that the design meets the desired criteria. If necessary, we will make revisions and adjustments to finalize the design.
