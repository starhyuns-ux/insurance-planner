# 💼 현빈 — 수익화, 가격, 비즈니스 전략·분석, KPI (최대 5개)

**CEO's Directive**

As the CEO of a one-person company, I hereby issue the following concrete and executable instructions:

**Goal:** Increase revenue by 20% within the next two weeks.

**Objectives:**

1. **수익화 전략**: Develop a new revenue strategy that focuses on increasing sales through targeted marketing campaigns and optimized pricing.
2. **가격 분석**: Conduct a thorough analysis of our product's pricing to identify opportunities for price adjustments, promotions, or discounts.
3. **비즈니스 전략**: Review and refine our business strategy to ensure alignment with the company's goals and objectives.

**KPIs (Key Performance Indicators):**

1. **Revenue Growth**: Monitor daily revenue growth and aim to increase it by 20% within the next two weeks.
2. **Conversion Rate**: Track conversion rates for marketing campaigns and optimize them to improve sales.
3. **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Monitor customer acquisition cost and aim to reduce it by 15% within the next month.

**Assignments:**

1. Assign the task of developing a new revenue strategy to Writer, with input from Researcher.
2. Assign the task of conducting a thorough analysis of our product's pricing to Designer, with input from Business Agent.
3. Review and refine our business strategy with all agents involved in this project.

**Deadline:** July 15th, 2026.

**Priority Level:** High

**Note:** Assign a priority level to each action item based on its importance and urgency.
