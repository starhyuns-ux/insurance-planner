# 📺 레오 —  YouTube 로드인 방식 마케팅 계획 정의

As the CEO of a 1-person company, I hereby issue a directive to create a marketing plan for YouTube login.

**Marketing Plan:**

Title: "Unlocking the Power of YouTube"

Objective:

* Increase brand awareness by 20% within the next quarter
* Drive traffic to our website by 15% within the next quarter

Target Audience:

* Young professionals aged 25-40 who are interested in entrepreneurship and innovation
* Target audience will be reached through a combination of social media advertising, influencer partnerships, and content marketing

Content Strategy:

* Develop a series of high-quality video content that showcases our company's mission, values, and products
* Publish content regularly to keep our audience engaged and informed about the latest developments in our industry
* Utilize YouTube's algorithm-friendly features such as cards, end screens, and annotations to increase engagement and visibility

Influencer Partnerships:

* Partner with 3-5 influencers who have a strong following in our target audience
* Influencers will create sponsored content that promotes our company and products
* Influencers will also share their experiences working with us on their social media channels

Paid Advertising:

* Run targeted ads on YouTube, Google AdWords, and Facebook to reach our target audience
* Ads will be designed to increase brand awareness, drive traffic to our website, and generate leads

**Task Allocation:**

1. **Leo**: Develop a series of high-quality video content that showcases our company's mission, values, and products.
2. **Coda**: Create sponsored content with 3-5 influencers who have a strong following in our target audience.
3. **Researcher**: Conduct research on our target audience to better understand their interests, behaviors, and pain points.

**Timeline:**

* Week 1-2: Develop marketing plan and content strategy
* Week 3-4: Create sponsored content with influencers and develop paid advertising campaign
* Week 5-6: Launch sponsored content and paid advertising campaign
* Week 7-8: Analyze results, adjust strategy as needed

**Evaluation Criteria:**

* Increase in brand awareness by 20% within the next quarter
* Increase in traffic to our website by 15% within the next quarter
* Engagement metrics such as likes, comments, and shares on social media platforms

This marketing plan aims to increase brand awareness, drive traffic to our website, and generate leads through a combination of content marketing, influencer partnerships, and paid advertising. By allocating tasks to each agent, we can ensure that our marketing efforts are focused and effective in achieving our objectives.
