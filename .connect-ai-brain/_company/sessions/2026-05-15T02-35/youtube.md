# 📺 레오 — 구체적으로 실행 가능한 YouTube 채널 분석 계획

As the CEO of Inshunut, I hereby issue a directive for a concrete and executable YouTube channel analysis plan.

**Task:** Conduct a thorough analysis of our YouTube channel to identify areas for improvement and optimize our content strategy.

**Objective:**

1. Analyze the current performance of our YouTube channel, including views, engagement, and conversion rates.
2. Identify key trends and patterns in our audience's behavior and preferences.
3. Develop a comprehensive plan to improve our channel's visibility, engagement, and overall performance.

**Action Plan:**

1. **Channel Analysis:** Run the `channel_full_analysis` script (located in `_tools/channel_full_analysis.py`) to gather insights on our channel's current performance, including views, engagement, and conversion rates.
2. **Audience Insights:** Use the `trend_sniper` script (located in `_tools/trend_sniper.py`) to analyze our audience's behavior and preferences, including their interests, demographics, and viewing habits.
3. **Content Strategy:** Develop a content strategy that takes into account the insights gathered from steps 1-2, including:
	* Identifying key themes and topics that resonate with our audience.
	* Developing a content calendar that aligns with our audience's preferences and viewing habits.
	* Creating engaging titles, thumbnails, and descriptions for each video.
4. **Implementation:** Assign this task to the `auto_planner` agent (located in `_tools/auto_planner.py`) to automate the process of creating new videos based on our content strategy.

**Timeline:**

1. Complete channel analysis within 24 hours.
2. Analyze audience insights and develop a content strategy within 48 hours.
3. Implement the content strategy using `auto_planner` agent within 72 hours.

**Metrics for Success:**

1. Increase in views and engagement on our YouTube channel.
2. Improvement in conversion rates (e.g., more leads, sales, or sign-ups).
3. Positive feedback from our audience and stakeholders.

By following this action plan, we will be able to optimize our YouTube channel's performance, improve our content strategy, and drive business results.
