As the CEO of Instagram, I'm pleased to provide a specific task that aligns with our current goals and objectives.

**Task:** Develop a new design for the Instagram feed, incorporating elements of storytelling, visual appeal, and engagement-driven content.

**Reasoning:** As we continue to evolve as a platform, it's essential to keep our feed fresh and engaging. By introducing a new design, we can create an immersive experience that encourages users to spend more time on the app, increasing their chances of discovering new content, interacting with friends and family, and exploring the vast array of interests and hobbies represented within the community.

**Target Audience:** This task is designed for our in-house designers (agents) who are familiar with our brand's visual identity and have experience working with various design tools. The ideal agents to assign this task to would be those who possess strong storytelling skills, a keen eye for detail, and an understanding of how to balance aesthetics with functionality.

**Deliverables:**

1. A visually appealing design concept that incorporates storytelling elements, such as:
	* A unique layout that guides users through the feed
	* Custom illustrations or graphics that highlight specific themes or topics
	* Innovative typography and font choices that enhance readability and emotional resonance
2. A comprehensive design document outlining the design decisions, including:
	* Design principles and rationales for each element
	* Technical specifications for implementation (e.g., CSS, HTML, etc.)
3. A prototype of the new feed design, with mockups or interactive prototypes demonstrating how it would work in practice.

**Timeline:** This task should be completed within 2 weeks, allowing our designers to showcase their creativity and expertise while meeting our goals for innovation and user engagement.

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**

1. Engagement rates: Measure the increase in likes, comments, shares, and saves on Instagram posts after implementing the new design.
2. User retention: Track the percentage of users who continue to use the app after introducing the new feed design.
3. Brand recognition: Monitor changes in brand sentiment and awareness through social listening and online reviews.

By completing this task, our designers will demonstrate their ability to balance aesthetics with functionality, creating an engaging and immersive experience for our users. This will not only enhance the overall user experience but also drive growth and loyalty within our community.
