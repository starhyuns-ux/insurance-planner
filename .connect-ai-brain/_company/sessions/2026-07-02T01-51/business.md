# 💼 현빈 — 수익화 전략 및 기여 분담 정의

**CEO's Instruction**

Define Revenue Strategy and Contribution Allocation

**Objective:** Develop a revenue strategy and allocate contributions to ensure the company's financial stability and growth.

**Scope:**

1. **Revenue Streams:** Identify potential revenue streams, including product sales, services, and partnerships.
2. **Contribution Allocation:** Allocate contributions from each agent to support the revenue strategy and optimize resource utilization.

**Guidelines:**

1. **Revenue Focus:** Prioritize revenue-generating activities and allocate resources accordingly.
2. **Cost Optimization:** Optimize costs by streamlining processes, reducing waste, and eliminating inefficiencies.
3. **Innovation:** Encourage innovation and experimentation to identify new revenue opportunities and stay ahead of the competition.
4. **Partnerships:** Foster strategic partnerships to expand reach, improve offerings, and increase revenue.
5. **Financial Planning:** Develop a comprehensive financial plan to ensure transparency, accountability, and informed decision-making.

**Assignments:**

* **Agent 1 (Business):** Define the revenue strategy and allocate contributions to support it.
* **Agent 2 (Finance):** Assist Agent 1 in developing a comprehensive financial plan and ensuring its implementation.
* **Agent 3 (Marketing):** Contribute to the development of marketing strategies to promote revenue-generating activities.

**Timeline:** 7 days
